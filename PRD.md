                                        Product Requirement Development
1) Problem: 
   A trekking company in Thamel where booking are tracked in the notebook and a WhatsApp group. The company have five staff and keeping the information in the notebook and WhatsApp chats causes lost of information, duplication of the booking, incorrect data, multiple booking in same date and no proper information about the trekker. The guide won't have idea about the upcoming trek lack of proper information channel.

2) Users: 
  i) Office Staffs(Primary User): The staff will make booking according to the request of the client. They add the details like client name, Trek route, number of people and other information as needed. They manage all the current booking to upcoming bookings.
  ii) Guides(Secondary User): The user will check the information of the trek and will know about the upcoming bookings.

3) V1 Features: 
 * Add a booking with the information like client name, trek route, number of people, paid(Yes/No).
 * View all the booking list.
 * View the upcoming list for the trek.
 * Can update and delete the list.
 * Mobile-friendly 


4) Out of Scope:
 * Notification and reminder
 * Guide will have more information about the trek not only just the upcoming booking
 * Improve authentication and security 
 * Role based access
