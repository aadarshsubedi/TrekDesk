A short list of questions I would ask the client before starting:
1) Does different staffs need different permission?(Is there any hierarchy in the staff)
2) Does there is any limitation in the booking fields like minimum number of people or individual can book the trek?
3) How far in advance a trek should be booked for the upcoming list?
4) Do the completed trek still be visible in the main list?
5) Is "Deposite paid or not paid" the only information the staff need to know or does the booking process have more payment stages? 
6) How comfortable are the office staff with using software day-to-day and should the interface assume zero experience or have some basic knowledge?
7) Do guides need this in no network area or can be work with limited network?
8) What kind of phone do guides use in their trek?


Assumption made in absence of client:
1) All the staff have equal access(No roled-based needed).
2) No there is no any limitation in the number of people for the booking individuals can also book.
3) The treks are assumed to be booked at least a few weeks in advance and the upcoming trek should be listed according to the soonest dates.
4) The past treks remains in the list rather than being archived.
5) Only "deposite paid or not paid" is enough for the payment status .
6) Office staff have basic comfort using app and the interface should be minialistic but doesn't need to be designed for someone with zero experience.
7) The guides just need to view the list so limited internet access is enough.
8) The guides use phone that are capable of running current browser.
